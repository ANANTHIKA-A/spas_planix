Most to-do lists are dumpsters for your anxiety. Planix is different. It is an intelligent, psychology-driven engine designed to dynamically hack your workflow.

The Philosophy

Traditional task managers assume you operate at 100% capacity all the time. We know you don't.

Planix bridges the gap between raw ambition and human fatigue by asking you how you feel, evaluating the difficulty and urgency of your tasks, and serving you exactly what you need to do next. When you are energetic, we push your hardest deadlines. When you are stressed or tired, we pull out simple, low-brainpower tasks to keep your momentum alive without causing burnout.
